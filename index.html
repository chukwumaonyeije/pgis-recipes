What PGIS Means Here

Performance Glycemic Intelligence System

PGIS frames recipes as experiments in metabolic stability, energy quality, recovery support, and practical food systems for daily life.

Presentation-first library

Each recipe is treated as a compact teaching deck: rationale, ingredient structure, execution protocol, and glucose-aware context in a slide-driven format.

Easy to maintain

New recipes can be added by dropping a Marp export into a recipe folder and updating one JavaScript data object on the homepage.
